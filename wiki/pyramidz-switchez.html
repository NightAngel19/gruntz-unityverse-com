PYRAMIDZ AND SWITCHEZ

Pyramidz are one of the fundamental puzzle mechanics of the game. They are Objectz designed to be used in association with Switchez, with the color of the Pyramid being the same as the color of the Switch (or Switchez) controlling it.

Checkpoint Pyramidz

Checkpoint Pyramidz are only ever activated once, when the requirementz of all their associated Checkpoint Switchez are satisfied. Checkpoint Pyramidz and Checkpoint Switchez that are in the same parent object are automatically connected to each other, not requiring any further manual work.

Green Pyramidz and Switchez

Green Switchez can be activated any number of times, and they toggle all of their associated Pyramidz at once. Green Pyramidz and Green Switchez that are in the same parent object are automatically connected to each other, not requiring any further manual work.

Red Pyramidz and Switchez

Red Switchez can be activated any number of times, and they toggle every Red Pyramid present on a Level. There is no need to connect any of them, if there are both Red Pyramidz and Red Switchez on a Level, they are all automatically associated.

Black Pyramidz and Switchez

Black One-Time Switchez are only ever activated once, and they toggle the associated Black Pyramidz at once. Black Pyramidz need to be assigned to exactly one BOTS to work. A single BOTS and all Black Pyramidz that are in the same parent object are automatically connected to each other, not requiring any further manual work.

Silver Pyramidz and Switchez

Silver Pyramidz need to be assigned to exactly one Silver Timer Switch. For each Pyramid, you can set a delay and a duration value. These dictate after how many seconds a Pyramid is raised after its Switch is pressed, and for how long it remains raised, respectively.

Orange Pyramidz and Switchez

Orange Switchez can be activated any number of times, and they toggle all of their associated Pyramidz and Orange Switchez at once. Orange Switchez and Orange Pyramidz that are in the same parent object are automatically connected to each other, not requiring any further manual work. There need to be at least two Orange Switchez present in the same parent object.

Purple Pyramidz and Switchez

Purple Switchez can be activated any number of times, when all connected Purple Switchez are activated at the same time, and they toggle all of their associated Pyramidz at once. Purple Switchez and Purple Pyramidz that are in the same parent object are automatically connected to each other, not requiring any further manual work. There need to be at least two Purple Switchez present in the same parent object.
