Scroll Gruntz are a newly discovered grunt type.
These gruntz will stand at a location, and when they sense your grunt they will go up to him and give him a scroll to read.
However, they also attack immediately after giving the scroll.
Unlike most gruntz, Scroll Gruntz will not chase your grunt until after they fully regain their stamina.

Puzzle Use: Using Scroll Gruntz is a tricky business, the only way to get your grunt to finish reading the scroll he is given is to give the Scroll Grunt a toy simultaneously. So be sure to provide the player'z grunt a toy to give! Note: giving the grunt a toy simultaneously is not as easy as it lookz, you will have to practice a couple of timez before getting the hang of it.
