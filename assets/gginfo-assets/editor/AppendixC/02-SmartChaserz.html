Smart Chaserz are exactly like Dumb Chaserz except for one major difference.

Smart Chaserz will ignore Gruntz that have stronger Toolz than they do.

Smart Chaserz will fight back if they are attacked, but Smart Chaserz are smart and will not chase Gruntz that have stronger Toolz than they do.

Smart Chaserz can be found carrying any of the Toolz in the game.
Unlike the specialized enemy Gruntz
(Brick Layer, Gauntletz, Goo Sucker, Digger, TimeBomber, Magic Wand and Scroller)
Smart Chaserz will not use their Toolz for anything other than fighting.

The range that a Smart Chaser can sense can vary.
