Front Candy
Edit Objects Dialog Box
Logic: FrontCandy
Image Set: Example: LEVEL_DANGER_SIGN


The discovery of FrontCandiez is credited to TurkeySandwich.
FrontCandiez behave just like any other EyeCandy object.
The difference is that they will alwayz be in front of anything, regardless of the object's height.
This can be most useful in the Miniature Masterz level set.
When you make a giant EyeCandy hole, anything put at it's base with be covered up.
With the FrontCandy logic, the object will show up in front of the giant EyeCandy.
