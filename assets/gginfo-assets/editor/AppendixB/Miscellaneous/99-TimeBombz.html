When the Rock is broken (or the Mound is dug up) the clock startz ticking, then ... Boom!

As an InGameIcon, its only value is at load time, to begin a chain reaction, or blow up something the Designer does not want revealed by just lookiing at the map.

Used as EyeCandy, this can really get the solver to move, even if it never goes off!
