The GunHat is a long-range weapon, capable of inflicting damage on an enemy Grunt from six Tilez away.

GunHatz Gruntz have the longest range of any weapon in the game.
Also, like a Sword Grunt, it only takez two 'hitz' from a cannonball fired by a GunHatz Grunt to turn an enemy into Goo.
Where a Boomerang Grunt is capable of doing harm to more than one Grunt at a time (even friendly Gruntz!), GunHatz only demolish one-half of a single Tile (that is, any Grunt on that Tile) at a time.
A GunHatz Grunt may only damage a moving Grunt by firing at a Tile where the enemy Grunt will be;
firing directly at a moving Grunt will result in a miss every time.
Missing can be fatal to a GunHatz Grunt, because it takez a long time to re-load.

If you are faced with battling a GunHatz Grunt, there is really little to fear; even a Bare-handed Grunt can easily defeat a GunHatz Grunt, by simply stepping out of the path of the cannonball and using 'hit and run' tactics.
If the enemy is a 'Hit And Runner' GunHatz Grunt, your task is a little more difficult, but I'll bet on your side every time!

Gunhatz also act as shieldz against ranged attackz, they will absorb only half the damage.

Missile speed: 1500 millisecondz to the target

Travel speed: 600 millisecondz per Tile ... 300 millisecondz per Tile with the Super Speed PowerUp.
