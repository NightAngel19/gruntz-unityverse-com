Gauntletz are heavy glovez made of metal ... much harder than Bare-handz. With Gauntletz, a Grunt can break Rockz (even Giant Rockz!)


, and most Brickz . Most? Yes, most! You see, Gauntletz cannot break Gold Brickz at all (it doesn't hurt the Gauntletz to try ... they just don't break). While Gauntletz are capable of breaking Black Brickz , the resultz are explosive(!) and alwayz fatal to the Gauntletz Grunt (unless he has an Invulnerability PowerUp). Gauntletz can break Red Brickz , but are broken themselvez in the process.

Gauntletz are among the most popular Toolz to be found in Gruntz puzzlez ... almost every Level, Standard or Custom, has at least one Grunt with this Tool. Close behind in popularity are Shovelz. Every Level eventually has a Warpstone Grunt ... but that is not much of a "Tool", so we won't include it in any popularity pollz.
