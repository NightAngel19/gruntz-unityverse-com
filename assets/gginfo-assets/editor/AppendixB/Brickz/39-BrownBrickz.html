A Bricklayer Grunt alwayz has a supply of Breakable (#39, Brown) Brickz ,
but it is sometimez useful to have something that will prevent an enemy Gauntletz Grunt from passing,
as an Unbreakable (#36, Gold) Brick ;
or take away his Tool, as a Gauntletz-breaker (#35, Red) Brick ;
or remove him from the area, as a Teleporter (#37, Blue) Brick ;
or remove him from the game entirely, as a Bomb (#38, Black) Brick ..

You may provide the solver with the meanz to acquire such a desirable Brick,
or (with this selection) just give him another ordinary Brick .
