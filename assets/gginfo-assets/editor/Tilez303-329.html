Typez of Brickz
Tile Properties/Attributes: User
Brown Brickz are normal Brickz, easily broken by a Gauntletz Grunt.
Note: for the Brickz listed below, you may need a TimeBombz or SuicideBomber Grunt .
Red Brickz are hardenedBrickz.They can be broken, but the Gauntletz break also.
Blue Brickz are easily broken, but cause a Teleportation effect.
Gold Brickz are super-hard Brickz, cannot be broken by Gauntletz, but nothing hurt.
Black Brickz are explosive Brickz, easily broken.
Every Grunt within one Tile of the Brick is killed when broken.
Brown Brickz are not always truly brown!
Brickz logics may be coded in such a way that the solver cannot see the true color of the Brickz; they all appear to be brown.
Many times you will see a Gruntz puzzle with a SpyGlass Grunt in it.More often than not, the reason is that brown Brick stackz contain some other color Brick; if there is more than one Brick in a stack, at least one of them is truly brown.Beware of Brickz of another color.The job of the SpyGlass Grunt is to reveal the true nature of the stack of Brickz!The true color of any Brick within two Tilez of the SpyGlass Grunt is revealed when he looks at a Brick with his SpyGlass.If you have a SpyGlass Grunt, put him to work revealing the true color of any Brick that your Gauntlet Grunt is tempted to break!
