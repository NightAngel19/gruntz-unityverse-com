The GruntCreationPoint is an object that is placed in a map that allows newly 'baked' Gruntz entry into the game. Its appearance is that of a "bullseye", with colored rings flashing from the outside edge to the very center, repeating constantly. In Battlez the GruntCreationPoints are owned by a player; player 1 cannot drop his/her Grunt onto player 4's (or 2's, or 3's!) GruntCreationPoint.
