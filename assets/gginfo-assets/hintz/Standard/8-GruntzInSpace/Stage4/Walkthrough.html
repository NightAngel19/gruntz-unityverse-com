Walkthrough Stage 4 (The final battle)

Pick up the gravity bootz and walk towards the green hold switch. When he's almost there the barehanded grunt walks towards the black one switch at the bottom. After hitting it he goes downstairs again and waits in front of the spikes. The bootz grunt hits the black one switch on the left and crosses the arrows towards the moving bridge. In the middle of the bridge just walk into the water towards the orange switch. Throughout this level you have to hit a total of six orange switches to reach the secret ? switch.

After hitting the switch walk back to the bridge, proceed to the checkpoint and pick up the sponge gun. First shoot the right enemy sponge grunt into the hole. Now walk to the single arrow near the orange switch. As the enemy sponge grunt is ready to shoot step on the arrow and when the shot arrives step on it again. You'll be shot onto the orange switch. Now you can shoot the left enemy sponge grunt into the hole. The barehanded grunt walks towards the checkpoint. Shoot him over the arrow to the gauntletz and together hit the checkpoint.

The gauntletz grunt crosses over the upper part of the breakable bridge and waits in front of the arrow. The sponge grunt sacrifices himself, he walks over the green toggle switch towards the end of the bridge and shoots on the arrow. The gauntletz grunt steps on the arrow and waits on the green hold switch until the rock has passed the green pyramid. Pass the pyramids and smash the four ice cubes, revealing a blue toggle switch. Hit the switch and proceed to the platform with the single enemy sponge grunt. Kill him, smash the ice cubes, get the zap cola and hit the black one switch. Walk back and proceed to the next bridge. Smash all the ice cubes, you'll find zap cola and two black one switches. After you've hit this switches, walk over the lava and the spikes and smash the hidden ice cube under the telescope. Hit the orange switch hidden under the ice, pass the rocks, hit the checkpoint and wait on the red hold switch.

Now continue with the two sword gruntz and the club grunt. Put the two sword gruntz onto the purple switches. The club grunt proceeds to the left green hold switch beneath the search light. Back to the gauntletz grunt. He goes upstairs, passes the search light and the lava. The club grunt now steps on the right green hold switch. The gauntletz grunt passes the last arrows and proceeds, via the stairs, to the coin and the zap cola. Hit the timer switch and proceed towards the stairs on the left. Pass the rocks and enter the blue warp. Hit the blue toggle switch and wait near the pool.

The club grunt walks back to the stairs, followed by the two sword gruntz. All three gruntz pass the rolling rocks, but only the sword gruntz cross the bridge. Lure one of the enemy gauntletz gruntz towards you and together stab him. When all gauntletz gruntz are killed, pick up the megaphone and give the toob tool to your gauntletz grunt. He swims around the island, gets the keg of zap cola, hits the black one switch and enters the blue warp. Hit the green toggle switch and step on the arrows just as the circling rock hits the upper row. Proceed towards the next switch, step back and then forward again. Now hit the green toggle switch in the middle of the next corridor, wait until the rock has passed the two-way arrow and get the gauntletz.

Step on the red pyramids when they are down, wait a moment, then on the spikes until the pyramids go down again. Smash the giant ice rock and hit the black one switch and smash the hidden ice cube, hit the orange switch and get the wingz. Walk around the space and hit the green toggle switch behind the rolling rock. Now walk towards the breakable bridge, position yourself at the right hand side and just click on the orange switch. Get the gauntletz and walk back. Get the goober straw, hit the checkpoint and wait.

Back to the sword gruntz, they go upstairs and downstairs again passing the rolling rocks. The easiest way is to walk along the holes and step on an arrow when the rocks are passing by. The club grunt, still waiting there, now joins his friends. All three gruntz proceed to the stairs, ignore the two enemy sponge gruntz for now. One by one lure the enemy gauntletz grunt towards you and stab them with the two sword gruntz. When you run out of zap cola you can also use the club grunt, but if you hit the enemy together he doesn't get the opportunity to hit you! Go upstairs and kill another two enemy gauntletz gruntz. Pick up the megaphone and give the shovel to the club grunt. The two sword gruntz are put onto the purple switches.

The shovel grunt kills the two enemy sponge gruntz. Then he proceeds towards the island, picks up the megaphone and waits on the green hold switch. Give the gauntletz from the resourcez to one of the sword gruntz. The gauntletz grunt smashes the giant ice rock and returns to the purple switch. The sword grunt picks up the shield and returns to the purple switches as well. Now the shovel grunt leaves the island again. Take all the zap cola and all three gruntz walk back towards the rolling rocks. Now the shovel grunt closes all the holes in the rolling rock area. You appreciate this later on in the level.. All three gruntz proceed to the checkpoint now.

The shovel grunt enters the moving pyramids maze. First hit the black one switch opposite the stairs. Next the ones in the center, then the upper right one and finally the one on the left. Leave the maze, open the holes under the rolling rocks and hit the black one switch beneath the search light. Now close the four holes and you're back at the beginning of the maze. Close the three holes in front of the black one switch and hit it. Walk to the right, pick up the megaphone and close the lower hole in front of the silver pyramids. The shield grunt and the gauntletz grunt proceed to the pyramids too. The straw grunt hits the timer switch for every grunt, one by one they walk through the pyramid maze.

Pick up the megaphone go downstairs and position your gruntz opposite the toy plates. The last grunt picks up another megaphone near the stairs. Give them all the proper toys. When the search light starts moving to the left, put the squeak toy grunt on the plate, then the yo-yo grunt. Finally hit the beach ball plate and move back immediately, also move your other gruntz back again. Now they all proceed towards the three purple switches. The straw grunt hits the black one switch and sucks up the four puddlez under the search light. Now return to the pyramids. One of the other gruntz hits the timer switch and the straw grunt walks through the pyramid maze.

Put the new grunt on the creation pad. He picks up the toob tool, the baby-walker and hits the black one switch. The shovel grunt proceeds to the platform with the four purple switches. He closes all the holes and waits on one of the switches. Now put all your other gruntz, except the gauntletz grunt, on the purple switches. The gauntletz grunt smashes the ice cubes, all containing a keg of zap cola, which he can reach without taking more than one keg. Now he walks towards the center, steps on the arrows and gets the resurrection spell. Use the spell and you got ten more gruntz! You should have a total of fifteen gruntz by now. One by one the gruntz take a keg of zap cola, get a tool and a toy. When you got all the toolz and toyz you're ready for the final battle!

Move all your gruntz to the final checkpoint. When you position your gun hat grunt near the entrance of the pyramid maze, you can already kill an enemy shovel grunt and wingz grunt from there. Now put him opposite the enemy gauntletz grunt and you can kill him too before the checkpoint goes down. When the battle is over you need at least seven gruntz to finish the game. You definitely need the gauntletz grunt, the shovel grunt and the sponge grunt! Position them on the last three plates. Put some tough guys in front, sword, club, rock and gun hat gruntz. Use your toyz and attack one enemy grunt with two of your gruntz. When the pyramids go down just let them come. Give one enemy a toy and together kill the other one. Don't cross the blocked pyramids until the first attackers have been killed! Then use the hit and run strategy to kill the enemy gun hat gruntz, finally kill the enemy shovel gruntz and the last gun hat grunt near the caste. Now hit the checkpoint and let the battle begin!

Put four gruntz on the purple switches around the castle. The shovel grunt enters the narrow corridor towards the telescope and hidden behind the third pile he digs up the last orange switch. Hit it, walk back and close the holes near the castle. One of the other gruntz picks up the warpstone and waits near the checkpoint. Now the gauntletz grunt collects the coins around the castle and smashes the hidden ice cube behind the last three piles. This reveals the secret ? switch which gives access to the red warp. After returning from the red warp put some gruntz on the four purple switches again. The gauntletz grunt walks back to the castle and smashes the center bricks of the lower row, he's killed. And now, for the very last time, give the warpstone piece to the king. You've finished the game!
