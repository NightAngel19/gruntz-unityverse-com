PYRAMIDZ

Pyramidz are one of the fundamental puzzle mechanics of the game. They are Objectz designed to be used in association with Switchez, with the color of the Pyramid being the same as the color of the Switch (or Switchez) controlling it.

By default, proper Pyramid-Switch associations are enforced. For example, you can connect Green Pyramidz only to Green Switchez (of any kind). Of course, you can override this behavior on a case-by-case basis, though it is not recommended.

Checkpoint Pyramidz

Checkpoint Pyramidz need to be assigned to one or more Checkpoint Switch(ez) and those same Checkpoint Switch(ez) need to be assigned to the Pyramidz as well to work as intended. The Pyramidz are only ever toggled once, when the requirementz of all associated Checkpoint Switch(ez) are satisfied.

Green Pyramidz

Green Pyramidz need to be assigned to at least one Green Switch (of any kind) to work. The Switchez toggle all of their associated Pyramidz at once.

Red Pyramidz

Every Red Pyramid on a Level is toggled at once by any single Red Switch (of any kind). You don't need to connect any of them, if there are both Red Pyramidz and Red Switchez on a Level, they are all automatically associated.

Black Pyramidz

Black Pyramidz need to be assigned to exactly one BOTS to work. The Pyramidz are only ever toggled once by a Grunt stepping on the associated BOTS.

Silver Pyramidz

Silver Pyramidz need to be assigned to exactly one Silver Timer Switch. For each Pyramid, you can set a delay and a duration value. These dictate after how many seconds a Pyramid is raised after its Switch is pressed, and for how long it remains raised, respectively.

Orange Pyramidz

Orange Pyramidz need to be assigned to at least two Orange Switchez. When an Orange Switch is pressed, it toggles all of its connected Pyramidz. Moreover, it toggles all other (at least one) Orange Switchez connected to it.

Purple Pyramidz

Purple Pyramidz need to be assigned to at least one Purple Switch to work. The Pyramid is toggled every time all of its associated Switchez are pressed.
